Our Founder

Logan Deyong — Founder, LODE Media

Logan Deyong

From a kid with a club to a creator with 20 million reach.

Logan picked up a golf club at the age of four. By eleven, he was winning. By eighteen, he had earned a place at a US university on a golf scholarship — the American dream was right there.

Then the world shut down.

COVID-19 cancelled everything. The visa, the flights, the dream. Logan was stuck at home like every other golfer on the planet. But instead of waiting it out, he picked up his phone.

He knew his fellow golfers were stuck at home too — millions of them, scrolling, searching for something to watch, something to learn. Logan started filming. Technique breakdowns. Swing drills. Course vlogs. Real content, shot at home, designed to help.

He went viral.

What started as a way to stay sharp became something far bigger. Instagram, TikTok, Snapchat — the audience grew fast. The numbers kept climbing. The brands started calling. The professional golf dream faded, but something more powerful took its place: the realisation that content was the game.

Puma. Sky. LIV Golf. PGA Tour. DP World Tour. Holland & Barrett. UPS. Some of the biggest names in sport and business saw what Logan saw — a young creator who could speak to a generation that traditional marketing couldn't reach. 91,000+ followers. 20 million+ combined reach. Content that didn't just get seen — it got shared, saved, and talked about.

Wentworth for the BMW PGA Championship. Dubai for the World Classic. Standing alongside McIlroy, Jon Rahm, Luke Donald. Not as a spectator — as a creator with a camera and a following that brands wanted access to.

Golf clubs started reaching out. Fly here. Come play. Film it. They had 18 holes of incredible content sitting on their doorstep and never thought to capture it. Logan brought the energy, the eye, and the excitement of actually being there.

In early 2026, Logan made the move. LODE Media Ltd was born.

Now he's taken years of content experience — the instinct for what makes a video break through, the understanding of algorithms, the knowledge of what turns followers into fans — and built an agency around it. A team of like-minded, talented individuals who share the same drive. Working with brands and businesses to expand their social media reach to audiences they've never touched before.

Logan's vision and energy is only just starting. An appetite to drive success — not just for himself, but for every brand he works with.
