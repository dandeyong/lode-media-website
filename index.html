About Us

A Team Built on Proven Results

LODE Media Ltd is a social media content management agency founded by Logan Deyong, a creator with an established history as a social media influencer whose reach and engagement exceeds 20 million.

We help brands, athletes, creators, and businesses grow their social media presence through consistent, high-quality content creation, strategic posting, and campaign planning. Our work is delivered using an in-house content and strategy approach, supported by a network of specialist photographers and videographers.

Having worked with brands including Puma, SKY, LIV Golf, PGA Tour, DP World Tour, Holland & Barrett, and UPS, we understand what it takes to cut through the noise and build audiences that last.
