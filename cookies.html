Cookie Policy

This cookie policy explains what cookies are, how LODE Media Ltd uses them on our website (lodemedia.co.uk), and how you can manage your cookie preferences.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to the website owner.

2. Cookies We Use

Our website uses only essential cookies that are strictly necessary for the website to function. We do not use any tracking, analytics, or advertising cookies.

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
None (first-party) N/A LODE Media does not set any first-party cookies on this website. N/A

3. Third-Party Cookies

Our website embeds content from third-party services that may set their own cookies on your device. We do not control these cookies.

Instagram Embeds (Meta Platforms)

We embed Instagram posts on our website to showcase our work and portfolio. When these embeds load, Instagram (operated by Meta Platforms) may set cookies on your device, including cookies used for:

  • Remembering your Instagram login status
  • Security and fraud prevention
  • Performance and analytics of their embed service
  • Personalisation and advertising (if you are logged into Instagram)

For more information about how Meta uses cookies, please visit Meta's Cookie Policy.

Google Fonts

We use Google Fonts to display typography on our website. Google may collect limited technical data when fonts are loaded, but this is not used for tracking purposes on our site. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

4. How to Manage Cookies

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View what cookies are stored on your device and delete them individually
  • Block third-party cookies
  • Block cookies from specific websites
  • Block all cookies
  • Delete all cookies when you close your browser

Here are links to cookie management instructions for common browsers:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Apple Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

Please note that blocking certain cookies may affect the functionality or appearance of our website, particularly the embedded Instagram content.
